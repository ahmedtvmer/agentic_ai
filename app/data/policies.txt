**Company Return Policy**
1. Items can only be returned if they are in "Delivered" status.
2. Items in "Shipped" or "Processing" status cannot be returned.
3. Refunds are processed within 5 business days after the returned item is received.
4. We do NOT offer direct replacements. You must return the item and place a new order.
5. Digital items (like gift cards) are non-refundable.
6. The return window is 30 days from the delivery date.
